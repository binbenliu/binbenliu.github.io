Bin Liu

 

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Management Information Systems of the Chambers College of Business and Economics at West Virginia University.

My research interests lie at the intersections between machine learning, healthcare, recommender systems, and privacy/security. I have published in premier journals such as IEEE TKDE, ACM TKDD, ACM TOPS, ACM TIST; and in top conferences such as KDD, AAAI, WSDM, USENIX Security. I received my PhD in Management (Information Technology track) from Rutgers Business School, Rutgers University.

Research Interests

  • Data Mining and Machine Learning

  • Health Analytics and AI for Healthcare

  • Recommender Systems

  • Privacy/Security
