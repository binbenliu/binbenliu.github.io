Bin Liu

 

I am a PhD student in the Department of Management Science and Information Systems at Rutgers Business School, Rutgers University. My advisor is Professor Hui Xiong. My research interests include:

  • Recommender systems, Location-based services, and Mobile Apps
  • Data mining and knowledge discovery, Business analytics, and Machine learning in general.

Contact: binben.liu@rutgers.edu
