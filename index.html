Bin Liu

 

I joined IBM Thomas J. Watson Research Center after receiving a PhD in Management (Information Systems track) from Rutgers Business School, Rutgers University under the supervision of Professor Hui Xiong.

My research interests lie at the intersection between technical and social aspects of information technology. I am interested in data mining/machine learning, and their intersections with recommender systems, healthcare, location/mobile, and security/privacy in social information systems.

I did interns at Samsung Research America, San Jose, CA, June – Aug. 2014; @WalmartLabs, San Bruno, CA, May – Aug. 2013; NEC Labs America, Princeton, NJ, May – Aug. 2012 (NEC Excellent Invention Award).
